Bill Gates

Relevant_Information+Quotes

Bill Gates is a co-owner in the Bill and Melinda Gates foundation, this foundation is the second largest charity in the world with around 36 billion dollars donated to areas that are less fortunate. Also helping to create vaccines for epidemics in other countries and for the covid 19 virus.

Bill Gates is a very large shareholder in these companies, Berkshire Hathaway, Four Seasons Hotel and Resorts, Canadian National Railway, FedEx, UPS and many more.

Bill Gates was considered the richest man in the world from 1995 to 2017 every year besides 2008 and from 2010 to 2013. In 1999 he became the first man to have 100 billion dollars.
