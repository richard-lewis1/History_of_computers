Bill Gates

General_Information

Name(s)

Bill Gates, William Henry Gates III

Years (born-dead)

October 28, 1955 (age 68 years), Seattle, Washington, United States

Bill Gates is one of the significant figures within the technological boom in the early 2000s and late 90s. Most commonly known as the founder of tech giant Microsoft, Gates is someone who led the charge into bringing the computer world to a whole new level. He can known as one of the greatest renowned entrepreneur, software developer, and philanthropist. His vision and influence have left a lasting impact on both the tech industry and humanitarian work.
