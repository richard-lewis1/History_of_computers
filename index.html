Years (born-dead)

In the early 2000s Bill Gates and Microsoft would release development tools such as Visual Studio and .NET framework making it a popular choice for developers of all levels within the technological world to this day to use. In conclusion, Bill Gates in collaboration with his company Microsoft but primarily himself, has created some of the first personal computer operating systems, designed high quality revolutionary computers and online tools for the computer science community to use for generations. His effect on technological advancement and more specifically computer science has changed the world as we know it today.
